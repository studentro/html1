The Importance of Wearing a Helmet

In this article I will try to convince you to wear a helmet. I will start by saying, TBIs (traumatic Brain injuries) are no laughing matter. Imagine your having a nice peaceful day out on the slopes, the wind is going through your hair without a care in the world. then out of nowhere you hit a sheet of ice, you are flung forward and agressively hit the ground. Everything goes blurry and your ears start to ring. If your lucky you'll end up with a mean headache, but not everyone is so lucky. The CDC reports that 166 people die from TBIs everyday. So do yourself a favor and protect your melon!
